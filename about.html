Every Scholar. Every Day. College Bound!

Danborough Group of Colleges is an extraordinary school of choice preparing scholars for academic challenges and success in higher education. Our accelerated curriculum educates, challenges, and inspires young people for their educational pursuits.

With two campuses strategically located in Glen View and Warren Park, we serve students from diverse backgrounds, fostering an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity while maintaining the highest academic standards.

Our Purpose & Vision

Committed to excellence in education and character development

Our Mission

To deliver quality education that empowers students to excel academically, develop strong character, and acquire valuable life skills in preparation for higher education and future success. We strive to create an environment where every student can reach their full potential through innovative teaching methods and comprehensive support systems.

Our Vision

To be the leading high school in Zimbabwe, recognized for academic excellence, holistic student development, and producing graduates who are well-prepared for university education and contribute positively to society. We envision a future where our alumni become leaders in their chosen fields.
