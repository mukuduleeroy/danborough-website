Excellence in Academic Programs

Danborough Group of Colleges provides comprehensive academic programs and related services to eligible students in accordance with the Zimbabwe Education Act. We ensure quality education in the least restrictive environment.

The academic department is committed to providing high-quality educational services to our students and families. Our faculties in Humanities, Business, and STEM offer dynamic programs designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed for success.

Our Academic Faculties

Three specialized faculties delivering comprehensive education

The STEM Faculty

The Science Faculty at Danborough Group of Colleges offers a comprehensive range of learning areas that foster scientific literacy, critical thinking, and problem-solving.

  • Established state-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities
  • Produced students who excel in national mathematics and science competitions
  • Developed partnerships with universities and research institutions
Mathematics Physics Chemistry Biology Geography Agriculture

The Humanities Faculty

Our Humanities Faculty is dedicated to developing critical thinking, communication skills, and cultural understanding through diverse academic disciplines.

  • Developed comprehensive library and resource centers
  • Produced students who win national essay writing competitions
  • Established cultural exchange programs for broader perspectives
English Language Literature History Religious Studies Shona/Ndebele Social Studies

The Business Faculty

The Business Faculty provides practical and relevant education that prepares students for the demands of the modern business world and entrepreneurship.

  • Developed practical curriculum for real-world business demands
  • Established partnerships with local businesses for internships
  • Produced students who win national business plan competitions
Accounting Business Studies Commerce Economics Entrepreneurship
