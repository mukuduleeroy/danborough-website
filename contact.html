About Danborough Group of Colleges

Danborough Group of Colleges is an extraordinary school of choice. We prepare students for the academic challenges that await them in college and beyond.

Our accelerated curriculum educates, challenges, and inspires young people for their higher educational pursuits. With multiple campuses across Harare, we provide quality education that meets international standards while addressing local educational needs.
