Simple Enrollment Process

Follow these easy steps to secure your place at Danborough

Choose Your Level

Select from ZJC, O Level, or A Level programs based on your academic background and goals.

Prepare Documents

Gather your birth certificate, previous academic records, and passport-sized photographs.

Submit Application

Complete our online application form or visit our admissions office to submit your application.

Complete Enrollment

Pay fees, attend orientation, and receive your welcome package to officially join our community.

Admission Requirements

What you need to join our academic community

ZJC Level

  • Grade 7 Certificate
  • Birth Certificate
  • 2 Passport Photos
  • School Transfer Letter
Age Requirement: 12-15 years

O Level

  • ZJC Certificate or Equivalent
  • Birth Certificate
  • 2 Passport Photos
  • Previous School Records
Age Requirement: 15-18 years

A Level

  • O Level Certificate (5 O Levels)
  • Birth Certificate
  • 2 Passport Photos
  • ZIMSEC Results Slip
Minimum: 5 O Level passes
