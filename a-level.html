Advanced Level (A Level) Education

Specialized education that prepares students for university admission and professional careers

What is A Level?

Our A Level program provides specialized education that prepares students for university admission and professional careers in their chosen fields. The curriculum offers in-depth study in selected subjects, allowing students to develop expertise and critical thinking skills essential for higher education.

Key Benefits:

  • Direct pathway to university admission
  • Specialized subject focus and research skills
  • International recognition and accreditation
  • Critical thinking and analytical skills development
  • Preparation for professional careers
  • Access to global educational opportunities

Assessment Structure

Understanding our A Level assessment methodology and examination timeline

AS Level (Year 1)

Foundation year building essential knowledge and skills for advanced study.

  • Foundation knowledge building
  • Mid-year assessments
  • AS Level examinations
  • Subject selection review

A2 Level (Year 2)

Advanced study with specialization and independent research components.

  • Advanced concept mastery
  • Independent research projects
  • Mock examinations
  • Final A Level examinations

Final Certification

Official certification and comprehensive support for university applications.

  • Cambridge/ZIMSEC certification
  • University application support
  • Career guidance sessions
  • Alumni mentorship program

Our assessment structure is designed to gradually build student confidence and competence, ensuring they are fully prepared for university-level study.
