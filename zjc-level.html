Zimbabwe Junior Certificate Excellence

Our ZJC program provides a solid foundation for students' educational journey, preparing them for advanced studies and future success.

Assessment & Progression

Our ZJC program uses continuous assessment to monitor student progress and prepare them for national examinations

Continuous Assessment

Regular tests, assignments, and projects to track progress and identify areas for improvement throughout the academic year.

National Examinations

Comprehensive preparation for official ZJC examinations with mock tests and examination technique training.

Progression to O Level

Seamless transition to Ordinary Level studies with strong foundational knowledge and study skills.

Parent Involvement

Regular progress reports and parent-teacher meetings to ensure collaborative support for student success.
